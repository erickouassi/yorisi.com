About Us


YORISI LLC is a legal entity formed in New York that provide websites plus business consulting services remotely over phone or videoconferencing. For the business consulting part, it is a service we offer, by suggesting tools and services to help you as well and teach you how to use them effectively. While we do not want to interfere with any practices you may already have in place, we will advise you how to incorporate small changes to fit with your new webapp or website.

Mission, Vision & values

Mission
To provide simple, good and easy to use websites and business consulting services to our clients based on their budget.

Vision
To be the trusted partner-of-choice for websites and business consulting services locally with expansion globally.

Values
"Keep it simple", "Do more with less" and "Learn by doing" are part of who we are and how we act.
